About Me

My name is Tsun-Yu Yang, a Ph.D. candidate from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I am supervised by Prof. Ming-Chang Yang. My primary research interests involve system designs in graph analytics and emerging memory/storage technology (e.g., non-volatile memory, ultra-low latency SSD, and Skyrmion racetrack memory).
